ABOUT ME

I'm an Engineer, who likes to think in its true sense. I consider that all the problems in the world are worth solving if it can make somebody's life a little easier and safer. I don't mind opening a tight jar of cookies for someone If it can put a smile upon their face. Neither would I mind working for a technology which can ensure a person's better health and safety in their long run.

Working in different areas of Engineering has made me more versatile, helped me go beyond my comfort zone and bring out the best in me. It has also made me understand the significance of working as a "Chief cook and Bottle-washer" in an organization, for its stability and successful deployment of projects.

I like working on new-age technologies. The field of Artificial Intelligence had always fascinated me. I believe that the idea of giving Knowledge of reasoning for a machine in helping us solve our problems will help mankind in its future endeavours. This interested me in working towards a fuzzy based project for my Master's degree Thesis work. The work was based on daylight harvesting (Please check out my portfolio section). I'm still getting my path through understanding KR2, Deep Learning, Bayes model... in reaching my goal of developing efficient, optimized control algorithms for a better living in this world for everyone.
